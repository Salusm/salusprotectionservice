How to be safe online?

To be safe online, one should have a unique and strong password, must have knowledge in securing their accounts, make sure to log out accounts when logged in from various devices, and one must think before they click.

Why is it important to know how to be safe online?

To make adults and children feel safe and secure when using social media.

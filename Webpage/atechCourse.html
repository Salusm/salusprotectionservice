Online Safety

The internet and our computers, smartphones and other devices are crucial to our way of life. Although the internet has many benefits, it also has some risks. Online safety is essential for both children and adults.

This page explains some of the risks and dangers online, and how you can keep yourself and your child safe.

It also tells you where to get more support and information on online safety and cybercrime.

How To Keep Your Children Safe

There are a wide range of dangers, including security and privacy issues, social problems for people that children may know in real life as well as strangers, and health concerns that can arise from internet usage. Parents of children of all ages should be concerned about internet safetyIf your child spends time online, you should understand the risks that could put them in danger

Online Dangers

Loss of Privacy

Sharing information in public that should be kept private; after information is posted online, it is outside your child's control

Inappropriate Content

Adult material, hate speech, violence, dangerous dares or challenges.

Cyberbullying

Bullying in the form of abusive and threatening texts or emails, messages on social media and chat rooms, or the posting of photos or video clips.

Online Grooming

Individuals targeting children through sexual coercion or extortion, usually on social media or gaming platforms

What People Need To Know About Social Media

What people need to know about social media Social media is a platform that has multiple platforms where users can post images, videos, and messages to communicate and interact with other users. Always be aware that social media has dangers like cyberbullying, identity theft, and hackers.

What adults need to know about age-inappropriate content The increasing age at which children become engaged in the digital world raises the possibility of them encountering something frightening. “Inappropriate” signifies different things to different people. Young children must be able to receive guidance from reliable people on what to do if they come into contact with improper content.

Online Safety Tips

Keep Personal Information Private.

Use strong passwords.

Install software updates.

Back up your personal data.

About Us

We are your Virtual Protectors

The Salus Protection Services aims to make a difference by providing engaging Online Safety training resources for parents, and children.
Our Certified School Program is a complete solution for schools to implement an effective approach to online safety.
It has been designed by education professionals to meet the statutory online safety safeguarding duties outlined in the Keeping People Safe in the Internet (KPSI) November 2022 guidance.

Our Mission

Mission

It is our mission to empower students, teachers, faculty and parents with the knowledge they need to keep themselves and their children safe online.

Vision

We aim to be the vanguards for the advocacy of online safety. We make learning exciting, fun and interactive while still ensuring that our courses are packed with all sorts of different information to empower the students, teachers, faculty and parents.

Goals

It is our goal to share information and tips about online safety to students, teachers, faculty and parents.
